What is the Cake Tax?

In my English class, there's one golden rule: we speak English! If you slip into another language after a friendly reminder, you get to choose your "penalty":

🎂 Bake a cake for the class
💪 Do 100 push-ups
✍️ Write a short essay on why speaking English in class matters

Most students go for the cake (no surprise there!). This booking system helps you pick your option and schedule your date.

🔒 Privacy Policy

What data is collected?

  • Name (to identify your appointment)
  • Class (to assign to the lesson)
  • Email address (only if you want reminders)

What is the data used for?

  • Display your booked appointment
  • Email reminders (3 days, 1 day, on the day - only with your consent)

How long is the data stored?

Your data will be automatically deleted 7 days after the booked appointment.

Your Rights

  • You can cancel your booking at any time
  • You can request deletion of your data
  • Contact: s.borgwardt@gso.schule.koeln

Voluntariness

Providing your email address is optional. You can also book without email and receive a calendar file for download.
